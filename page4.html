ENHYPEN is a Korean boyband under BELIFT Lab. This group consists of seven members: Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-Ki. They are the finalists from the survival show "I-LAND." This competition aired from June 2020-September 2020, and they debuted on November 30, 2020. Just like the hypen (-) that connects one word to another, ENHYPEN means "Connection, Discovery, & Growth." The seven members are able to do these three wonderful things with each other, their music, and their fans.
